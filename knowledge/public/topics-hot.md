## Hot topics (Audi A4 B7 2.0 TDI BLB) — shortlist

Notă: subiectele de mai jos sunt extinse în fișiere separate din `knowledge/public/` (mai ușor de indexat și de citat).

1) **P0299 Underboost / limp mode**
- Vacuum leaks, N75, VNT actuator, boost leaks.
- Source: `https://www.audi-sport.net/xf/threads/259200/`

2) **Boost pipe / intercooler seal leaks**
- O-rings + spring clips are a common leak point; oil mist at joints.
- Source: `https://www.audi-sport.net/xf/threads/111698/`

3) **P0101 / MAF diagnosis with VCDS**
- Group 003, compare specified vs actual; “static” or <500 mg/str often points to MAF issues.
- Source: `https://help.idparts.com/help/testing-for-a-faulty-maf-w-vcds-on-a-tdi`

4) **ABS 00290 (G46 rear left)**
- Electrical fault vs signal out of spec; wiring vs sensor vs air gap/pickup ring.
- Source: `https://wiki.ross-tech.com/index.php/00290`

5) **Oil pump / balance shaft hex drive (catastrophic risk)**
- Known high-impact failure mode on some 2.0 TDI variants; treat low oil pressure warnings as stop-now.
- Source (forum discussion): `https://www.vwaudiforum.co.uk/forum/archive/index.php/t-71723.html`

6) **Tandem pump / vacuum supply issues**
- Can contribute to vacuum-related faults (brake pedal hard, boost control issues).
- Source (forum): `https://www.vwaudiforum.co.uk/forum/archive/index.php/t-170122.html`

7) **EGR cooler / coolant loss**
- Coolant loss without visible leaks; possible internal leak to exhaust.
- Source (discussion): `https://www.vwaudiforum.co.uk/forum/archive/index.php/t-198758.html`

8) **Glow plug light flashing + limp mode**
- Often accompanies engine management faults; always scan with VCDS and log conditions.
- Source: `https://www.audi-sport.net/xf/threads/126184/`

9) **Injector issues / injector loom (symptoms: rough idle, misfire, diesel smell)**
- More common on some 2.0 TDI PD variants; still a “hot” topic for troubleshooting.
- Source: `https://www.audi-sport.net/xf/threads/216867/`

10) **Crank sensor / no-start intermittently**
- Hot-start/no-start is a recurring theme; wiring can mimic sensor failure.
- Source: `https://www.audiownersclub.com/forums/topic/17161-a4-b7-20tdi-brd-engine-crank-sensor-issue/`

11) **Coolant temperature sensor / thermostat symptoms**
- Slow warm-up, gauge behavior; distinguish sensor vs thermostat.
- Source: `https://www.audiownersclub.com/forums/topic/20388-how-many-coolant-temp-sensor-are-there-in-audi-a4-b7-20-tdi-bre-2006/`

12) **ABS wheel bearing pickup ring / rust/air gap**
- Rust can increase air gap; symptoms similar to sensor failure.
- Source: `https://wiki.ross-tech.com/index.php/00290`
